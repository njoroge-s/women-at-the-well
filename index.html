About Us

Our Mission

At "Women At the Well," we are a community of young Christian women dedicated to nurturing our spiritual growth, deepening our connection with God, and supporting each other on our faith journeys. Our mission is to create a safe and welcoming space where we can come together, share the Word of God, engage in heartfelt prayer, and uplift one another.

Who We are

"Women At the Well" is the realization of a shared vision among a group of passionate women seeking to foster spiritual growth in a supportive and empowering environment. Our diverse backgrounds and life experiences enrich our discussions, prayers, and reflections, allowing us to learn from one another and grow together.

Our Values

  • Faith: We center our lives around Christ and His teachings, seeking to strengthen our faith through scripture, prayer, and communal worship.
  • Unity: We believe in the power of unity among women, standing together in both our joys and challenges.
  • Spiritual Growth: Our pursuit of spiritual growth is a lifelong journey. We embrace opportunities for personal development and understanding.
  • Encounter with God: We strive to create an atmosphere where encounters with God are not just moments but a way of life, where we feel His presence in every aspect of our existence.

What We do

Through regular meetings, workshops, retreats, and online discussions, we engage in meaningful conversations, study scripture together, share personal testimonies, and offer each other encouragement and support. Our events are designed to provide opportunities for us to delve into the Word, strengthen our prayer lives, and experience God's transformative love.
